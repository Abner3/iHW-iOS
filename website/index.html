Easy Setup

You don't even need to type in your schedule!

Simply log into HW.com and your classes automatically downloaded into the app.

iHW knows when special schedules, exams, and holidays happen so you never need to enter them manually.

Features

  • View your class schedule for any day of the year
  • Add notes and to-do items to any period
  • See how much time is left until next period
  • Works with both Middle School and Upper School
